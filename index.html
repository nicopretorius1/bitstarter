I want to produce a social media aggregator, but focused on bringing headline news to customers, not just the normal aggregator type application. In order to do this I will redesign the interface that customers will use to follow their social media channels. The interface will focus on the graphic display of important events and hashtag summerisation. Instead of seeing all the messages, rather customers will be able to see where messages originates and what is the mood of messages. The customer can then make a more informed decision on which tags and events they want to follow.

We offer the following value propsition for our different customer segments:

  1. Convenience: this value proposition applies to all our users, they will be able to better aggregate and follow a single or multiple social media accounts. They will be able to see trends and customize their feeds to provide a better user experience
  2. Usability: mobile users will be able to use our mobile app that will give them a better intetegrated invironment where they can interact and collaborate easier on their mobile news feeds.
  3. Customization: for paying customers we provide a data analysis service, they will be able to see the different analytical views of their social media feeds providing a better level of detail and understanding of their social feeds.
